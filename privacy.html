Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

In Short

Daysift stores everything locally on your device. We don't have servers storing your data. We don't have accounts. We don't track you.

The only data that leaves your device:

  • Search queries are sent to OpenAI when you use AI features
  • Domain names are sent to Google to fetch website icons
  • Payment info is processed by Lemon Squeezy, a trusted payment platform used by thousands of software companies — we never see your card details

What We Collect

Data Stored Purpose
Browser history (URLs, titles, timestamps) Locally on your device Enable search
Notes you create Locally on your device Save your notes
Pinned items Locally on your device Quick access
Settings & preferences Locally on your device Remember your choices

What Leaves Your Device

Data Sent to When Why
Search query text OpenAI You use AI search Provide AI answers
Domain names Google Viewing results Display favicons
Payment details Lemon Squeezy You upgrade Process payment

We never see your payment information — Lemon Squeezy handles this entirely.

What We DON'T Collect

  • No accounts or login
  • No email addresses (unless you contact us)
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No passwords or form data
  • No page content
  • No cloud storage of your data

Your Control

  • See your data: It's on your device — open browser dev tools
  • Delete everything: Uninstall the extension
  • AI queries: Not stored by us; see OpenAI's privacy policy

Third-Party Services

Service What they receive Their policy
OpenAI Search queries (AI features) openai.com/privacy
Google Favicon API Domain names only google.com/privacy
Lemon Squeezy Payment details lemonsqueezy.com/privacy

Security

Your data lives in your browser's local storage. We can't access it. External transmissions (AI queries) use HTTPS encryption.

Changes

We'll update this page if anything changes. Major changes will be noted in the extension update notes.
